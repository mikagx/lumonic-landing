The challenge

Private equity operations are stuck in the dark ages.

Fragmented portfolio data

Every platform company reports differently. Your team spends weeks normalizing data across Excel files, board decks, and accounting systems before a single number is useful.

Manual roll-ups

Consolidating financials across platforms and add-ons is a quarterly fire drill. One wrong formula and your entire roll-up is off.

Slow board reporting

Deal teams spend more time building PowerPoints than analyzing performance. Board packs are late, inconsistent, and immediately out of date.

No real-time visibility

By the time you see the numbers, they are already two months old. You cannot act on stale data, but that is all you have between quarterly cycles.

The solution

Modern portfolio management, built for private equity

Unified data platform

All portfolio company data in one place — normalized, validated, and ready to analyze regardless of how each company reports.

AI-powered analysis

Ask questions in plain English. Get answers sourced directly from portfolio company documents with full traceability.

Portfolio reviews

Generate IC-ready portfolio reviews with live data. Compare performance across platforms, add-ons, and time periods instantly.

Pro forma tracking

Track actual performance against the deal model. See exactly where value creation is on track and where it is falling behind.

Restatement detection

Board-ready reporting

One click to generate board packs, LP updates, and fund-level summaries. Always current, always consistent, always on time.
