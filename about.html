OUR MISSION

Focus your team on investing, not data collection

Investment professionals spend too much time chasing documents, manually spreading financials, and reconciling data across systems. Lumonic automates the grunt work so your team can focus on what actually matters — making better investment decisions.

Our platform combines AI extraction with human QA to deliver institutional-grade accuracy on every data point. Every number traces back to its source document with a full audit trail, so you never have to second-guess your data.

Lumonic ingests any format — PDFs, Excel files, scanned documents, even handwritten notes — and transforms them into structured, verified data you can act on immediately.
